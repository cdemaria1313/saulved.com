About Bio-Graphene Global

A science-led company rethinking how graphene is made—and what it can do.

Our Mission

Redefining Graphene from the Ground Up

Bio-Graphene Global was founded on a simple but consequential idea: that the world's most promising advanced material doesn't have to come at an environmental cost.

Traditional graphene production relies heavily on graphite—a resource under increasing global pressure as demand from electrification and EV manufacturing accelerates. We took a different path. By developing a clean, controlled process that converts 100% organic source materials into high-performance graphene, we've built a production model that is sustainable from its first step to its last.

Our mission is to lead the transition toward graphene solutions that help industries perform better and impact the planet less—not as a trade-off, but as the same outcome.

What We Do

Advanced Graphene. Responsibly Made.

We develop and produce biographene—high-quality graphene derived entirely from organic inputs—and apply it in industrial markets where performance and sustainability matter most.

Production

Our patent-pending thermal-mechanical conversion process transforms organic source materials into consistent, high-quality biographene with zero by-product waste and a high degree of process control.

Products

Our biographene is available in multiple forms—powders, concentrated aqueous solutions, and graphene-enhanced coated materials—enabling broad application across industries.

Applications

We initially focus on concrete and asphalt, where our materials deliver measurable performance improvements and meaningful CO₂ reductions. We are actively expanding into energy storage, coatings, electronics, and advanced polymers.

Research

Our Innovation Centre in Spokane, WA drives ongoing R&D into new synthesis methods, product forms, and market applications—including next-generation in-situ graphene growth technology.
